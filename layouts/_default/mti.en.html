Mewah Tek Indonesia
COMBUY, a cross-border e-commerce platformunder MTI, was first launched on November 1, 2024, with a focus on the Indonesian market.
Its business model primarily involves acquiring customers through social platforms, operating private domains, and adopting a group-buying model, where "the more people buy, the lower the price". This model aims to reduce product costs through large-scale procurement and sales, thereby providing consumers with low-priced goods.

Price Advantage

Product diversity

Intl Strategy

COMBUY's supply chain is globally oriented, providing one-stop entry services for overseas suppliers to swiftly tap into the Indonesian marker.

Tech & Ops

COMBUY adopts advanced technology and operational strategies, such as a semi-self-managed model, granting merchants more autonomy while providing comprehensive platform support.
